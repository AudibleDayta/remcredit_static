How It Works

Apply Online

Fill out our simple application form to get started. We assess your eligibility based on your remittance history and provide instant approvals for a RemCredit card.

Once approved, you receive a RemCredit virtual card with an approved credit limit. Now you can take care of the emergency.

Send Remittance

Use your RemCredit card to send money to your loved ones abroad using any digital remittance platform.

Flexible Repayment

Pay down your credit balance to free up your limit and enjoy flexible repayment options tailored to your remittance frequency.

At RemCredit, we understand that financial flexibility is crucial during times of emergency. Our unique approach to repayment empowers you to manage your credit balance at your own convenience.
